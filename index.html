Who Are We?

Over 200 Years of Quality

We scour the land for the best ingredients for our potions, measure them in exacting amounts, and mix them all together to bring you nothing but the best of the best. We accomplish this using our patented triple spirit distillation, alongside a proprietary arcane heating process to preserve the most subtle of magical effects and flavours. All of this is finally carefully poured into our elementally annealed bottles, to make sure they don't crack under pressure when you won't either.

Our History

26th Sun's Dusk, 7E 210

The Year Without

Following the events of the end of The Great Thaumatic War, Viric Metan plied their alchemical skills to help with the relif efforts during the disaster, saving countless lives and securing a plot of land in the Workshop District of Choris by government appointment.

14th First Seed, 8E 3

Early Successes

After the end of The Year Without, Viric's buisness began to boom with the reconstruction efforts due both to their reputation from their aid during The Year; and additionaly due to their effective and unique potions: The 11 special brews from which eventually drew name.

3rd Frost Fall, 8E 34

Merger with Tol's Tinctures

11 years of buisness later, Metan's ability and the quality of their wares were household knowledge, with everyone from paupers to princes using their wares. But there was an issue, Metan couldn't produce potions fast enough, even with a brace of capable apprentices. To remedy this Metan approached Immenarith Tol, Owner and proprietor of Tol's Tinctures, a similar company that had met with less acclaim, but was more able to deal with the bulk orders. After some negotiation, a deal was brokered and the two companies merged, creating the Metan-Tol Alchemy Group.

30th Mid Year, 8E 52

Our First New Branch

In 8E 51, Effective transportation between the city-states was finally re-stablished and after much petitioning, a buisness liscence was granted to MT Alchemy to operate in Forgeheim, in exchange for a discounted supplying deal with the Dwarven Military.
